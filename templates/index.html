A little about us

We're two close friends, who eventually got burnt out of seeing apps, which did "unnecessarily more" than they're supposed to do. We really wanted to challenge this common principle by building our own apps.

So, as any knowledgable developer would've done - we started to code.

However, both of us were backend and systems developers, and had little to no prior knowledge of app development. How could we even pull this off?

Why you ask? Because,

We have one, clear mission.

“Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works.”
- Steve Jobs
Now what's so fascinating about this quote? It's because, as developers advance more through tech each year, the design principles even the basic apps follow become more "scattered". That's what we're here to solve - make minimal apps which "just work".
